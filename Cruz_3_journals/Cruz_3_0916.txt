The three guiding rules/princeples for the class are tinker, talk to others,
and no electronics.

If a student has a problem with a code then they are to tinker with it or 
ask a neighbor for their help. 

The steps of the problem solving process:
1)Define the problem 
2)Generate solutions
3)Decide a course of actions
4)Implement the solution
5) Evaluate the solution
